Welcome to Contractors Register

Contractors Register is a tool for managing and monitoring the records of contractors, visitors, and workers in your organization.

Main Features

Contractor Management

Register and track all contractor information efficiently.

Visitor Registration

Monitor visitors entering and leaving the premises.

Worker Control

Manage the activities and presence of workers in real-time.

How to Use the System

Follow the steps below to manage records:

  • Adding Records

    To add a new record, navigate to the appropriate section (Contractors, Visitors, Workers) and click on the button.

  • Updating Records

    To update an existing record, find the record in the list and click on the button next to it.

  • Deleting Records

    To delete a record, find the record in the list and click on the button next to it. Note: Deletion may be restricted if the record is referenced by other entries.

If you do not see the options to add, edit, or delete, it is likely that you do not have the necessary permissions. Please contact your administrator for access.
